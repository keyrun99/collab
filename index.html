Brief Project Overview

3D Utility & Infrastructure Planning at Ontario Parks

Ontario Parks, a division of the Ministry of the Environment, Conservation and Parks in Ontario, Canada, safeguards numerous natural and cultural resources across parks and protected lands, while also facilitating recreational and educational activities. Our project aims to model these assets to improve utility and infrastructure maintenance, demonstrating the benefits of digitized resources for all parks. With our 3D model, our team hopes to engage park management and maintenance staff by providing an interactive experience of park resources - starting with Emily Provincial Park as an example.
